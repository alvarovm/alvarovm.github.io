ALCF cookbook

Foreword

My intent with this notes is to collect some useful information
to use ALCF computers and to make easier the slope on
the learning curve and to get ready faster for massive
calculations in BG/Q and KNL/Cray computers.


Just a guide

I am committed with the success of every INCITE or
ALCC project in DOE leadership facilities. I will try to
keep these notes/wikis updated as much as possible.
I will appreciate your feedback to improve the content
and the accuracy of the herein text.

This documentation is offered "as is" and it is not an
ALCF official channel of communication, and overall,
it should not be taken as a definitive text guide.
